Wordle Solver
How To Use
X
  • Using the word automatically suggested by the solver, or your own word, perform a guess in Wordle.
  • In the solver, input your guess using the keyboard or dropdown menu, and toggle the tiles to match the colours observed in Wordle.
  • Click Submit, and the solver will suggest the next optimal guess.
  • Repeat the steps outlined above, until you complete the game.
  • You can undo your last guess or restart at any time.
